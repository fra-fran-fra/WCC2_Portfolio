Francesco Angelini

Hey, this is Francesco. I produce and compose with sound. Central to my practice is finding meaningful ways to bring contrasting elements together. Using digital processing techniques, I break up recorded sounds and use them to write new musical phrases. The gestures that characterise my works are a product of both computational randomness and human intentionality.
